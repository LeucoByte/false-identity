# Reasons for job termination/change
# Format: Reason
# Used for previous jobs when person changed employment

# Voluntary reasons (positive/neutral)
Better job opportunity
Career advancement
Relocation
Pursuing further education
Starting own business
Family reasons
Salary increase elsewhere
Career change
Seeking new challenges

# Company-related reasons (neutral)
Company restructuring
Company closure
Department elimination
Contract ended
Temporary position ended
Project completion
Company relocation
Merger or acquisition

# Neutral/circumstantial
End of internship
Seasonal work ended
Fixed-term contract expired
Moved to different city
Health reasons
Retirement (for elderly)

# For students/interns
Internship program completed
Returned to studies
Graduated and found full-time position

# Economic reasons
Economic crisis layoff
Position eliminated
Cost-cutting measures
Business downturn
